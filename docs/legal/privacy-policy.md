# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 9, 2026**

## Introduction

DYDYD ("Did You Do Your Dailies?") is a gamified habit tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Display name (optional)
- Profile avatar URL (optional)

### Usage Data
- Quest completions (timestamps, categories, XP earned)
- Streak data and progress statistics
- Badge achievements
- App settings and preferences

### Health Data (Optional)
If you grant permission, we may collect health metrics from:
- **Apple Health (iOS)**: Steps, sleep duration, workout minutes, mindful minutes, water intake, heart rate, stand hours
- **Google Fit (Android)**: Steps, sleep duration, workout minutes, calories

Health data is used solely to auto-complete matching quests and is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- Push notification token (for sending reminders)
- Timezone (for scheduling and streak calculations)

## How We Use Your Information

- **Core Functionality**: Track habits, calculate XP, maintain streaks, award badges
- **Notifications**: Send daily reminders and achievement alerts (with your permission)
- **Analytics**: Improve the app experience (aggregated, non-identifying data only)

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Share individual health data with any third party

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
- API communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- JWT tokens expire after 15 minutes (access) and 7 days (refresh)

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
- Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytics

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data associated with your account
- **Correction**: Update your profile information at any time
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data from within the app
- **Portability**: Request an export of your data
- **Opt-out**: Disable notifications, health data sync, or any optional features

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Push notification delivery
- **Apple HealthKit / Google Fit**: Health data integration (opt-in only)
- **Sentry**: Error monitoring (no personal data included in error reports)

## Children's Privacy

DYDYD is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification or email.
